About

Hey, you entered the domain of Mohammad Saadeh(game designer/programmer, resilient worker, and MMO enthusiast.) In here you can view my resume, and a few of my projects and 3D models created on Maya.

I am currently a student at the University of Central Florida, majoring in Digital Media. I have made a few projects on my free time and others on a team. I also have some work on 3D modeling using Maya. I specialize in gameplay programming and level design but enjoy working any problem in my way. I would like to chat about job opportunities, my projects, or any topic you have.
